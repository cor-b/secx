Threat-hunting

Proactively hunting threats: indicators of attack, behavior analysis, internal and external intelligence, detection enablers, threat intelligence platforms, IoC sharing, rule-based languages, and counterintelligence.

overview

Domains 2 & 4 OBJ 2.3 / OBJ 4.3: integrate controls into a secure architecture and apply threat-hunting and threat intelligence concepts. Topics span indicators of attack (TTPs), behavior and data analysis (recon, hypothesis-based searches, UBA), internal intelligence (adversary emulation, honeypots/honeynets), detection enablers (sensors, monitoring, alerting, logging), external intelligence (OSINT, ISACs, Admiralty Scale, dark web), threat intelligence platforms, IoC sharing (STIX/TAXII/AIS), rule-based languages (Sigma/YARA/Rita/Snort), and counterintelligence/operational security.
