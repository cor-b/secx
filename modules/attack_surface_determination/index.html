Attack Surface Determination

Mapping every way in: the technical, operational, organizational, and cloud attack surfaces, how change introduces new exposure, and modeling threats with and without an existing system.

overview

Domain 1 (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) OBJ 1.4: perform threat modeling activities. Topics span the technical attack surface (architecture reviews, data flows, trust boundaries, code reviews), operational (user factors, unsanctioned assets/accounts), organizational (third-party connections, public digital presence), and cloud attack surfaces (internal/external assets, services discovery, malicious daemons), the organizational change attack surface (staffing, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures), and modeling threats with and without an existing system.
