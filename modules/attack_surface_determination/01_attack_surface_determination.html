Attack Surface Determination

Mapping every way in. The attack surface spans technical, operational, organizational, and cloud dimensions, shifts during change, and is modeled differently for systems that exist versus ones still on the drawing board.

overview

Domain 1 (Governance, Risk, and Compliance), Objective 1.4: perform threat modeling activities. Assessing the attack surface, all potential entry points, lets you protect key assets. It spans technical, operational, organizational, and cloud dimensions, shifts during organizational change, and can be modeled both with and without an existing system. The section covers each of these.

area 01

The Dimensions

Technical (infrastructure), operational (people/processes), organizational (external relationships), and cloud attack surfaces.

area 02

Change

Organizational change attack surface: staffing changes, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures.

area 03

Modeling

Modeling with an existing system vs modeling without one (building security in from the ground up).
