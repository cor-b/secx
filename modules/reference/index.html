Cross-cutting study aids that pull from every section: one place to look up an acronym, a term, or compare similar concepts side by side.

overview

These pages are not lessons, they are fast-lookup references built on top of all the sections. The glossary collects every acronym and key term in one searchable place. The cheat sheets line up the easily-confused concepts (access models, testing types, frameworks) in side-by-side comparison tables for exam-day recall.
