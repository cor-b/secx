authentication factors

factorcategoryexamplesweakness
Something you knowknowledgepassword, PIN, security questionphishable, guessable, reusable
Something you havepossessionphone OTP, authenticator app, token, smart cardcan be lost or stolen
Something you areinherencefingerprint, face, iris, voicespoofable; cannot be changed

MFA requires two or more factors from different categories. A password + PIN is still single-factor (both are "know"). A smart card + PIN is two-factor (have + know).
