Detection and Mitigation

Building systems that withstand and respond to attack: detecting tampering, designing security in, validating input, writing safe code, controlling access, protecting data, patching, and failing gracefully.

overview

Domains 3 and 4, Objectives 3.4 (implement hardware security technologies/techniques) and 4.2 (analyze vulnerabilities/attacks, reduce the attack surface). This section pairs with Vulnerabilities and Attacks: it's the defensive counterpart. Topics: tamper detection and countermeasures, design mitigations, validation mitigations, safe functions, access control mitigations, confidentiality management, update management, and fail-safe mechanisms.

area 01

Detect & Design

Tamper detection and countermeasures, design mitigations (patterns, defense-in-depth, dependencies), validation mitigations.

area 02

Code & Access

Safe functions (atomic, memory-safe, thread-safe) and access control mitigations (least privilege, least functionality, allowlisting).

area 03

Protect & Recover

Confidentiality management (indexing, keys, encryption, code signing), update management, fail-safe mechanisms.
