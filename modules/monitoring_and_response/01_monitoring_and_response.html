Monitoring and Response

Analyzing data to detect and respond to threats: aggregating and correlating logs, enriching with threat intel, baselining normal behavior, running it all through a SIEM, then alerting, prioritizing, and reporting.

overview

Domain 4 (Security Operations), Objective 4.1: analyze data to enable monitoring and response activities. Continuous analysis against normal behavior detects anomalies; threat intelligence and logs reveal emerging risks; alerting flags issues in real time; and prioritization keeps efforts focused. The section covers aggregate data analysis, threat intelligence sources, system log sources, vulnerabilities/data security, behavior baselines, SIEM event and data management, alerting, alert prioritization, and reporting/metrics.

area 01

Collect & Enrich

Aggregate data analysis, threat intelligence sources, system log sources, vulnerabilities and data security.

area 02

Analyze

Behavior baselines and analytics, SIEM event management, SIEM data management.

area 03

Act & Report

Alerting, alert prioritization factors, reporting and metrics (visualization, dashboards).
