Secure Architecture Design

Integrating security controls throughout system design to protect data across its lifecycle, manage hybrid environments, and reduce exposure to threats.

overview

Domain 2, Objective 2.3: given a scenario, integrate controls in the design of secure architecture. Topics cover data protection across all states, classification and labeling frameworks, data loss prevention, hybrid and third-party infrastructure complexity, attack surface reduction, and measuring control effectiveness.
