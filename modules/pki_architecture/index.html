PKI Architecture

The public key infrastructure that underpins digital trust: managing, issuing, validating, and deploying certificates.

overview

Domain 2 (Security Architecture) OBJ 2.4: apply security concepts to access, authentication, and authorization design. Topics cover certificate management (types, extensions, formats), certificate authority functions (CAs, chain of trust, RAs, CSRs), certificate validation (OCSP stapling, CRL, pinning), and certificate deployment (templates, automation, integration).
