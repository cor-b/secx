Access, Auth, Authz

Designing systems that verify identity, enforce access decisions, and maintain oversight — from physical controls and access models to federated identity and tamper-proof audit trails.

overview

Domain 2, Objective 2.4: given a scenario, apply security to the design of access, authentication, and authorization systems. Topics cover physical and logical access control mechanisms, the full access provision lifecycle, rule-based and role-based access models, federated identity and SSO protocols, conditional access policy architecture, and logging and auditing for compliance.
