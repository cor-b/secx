Specialized System Security

Securing the systems that run the physical world: embedded and IoT devices, operational technology, and the obsolete legacy gear that can't simply be patched.

overview

Domain 3 (Security Engineering), Objective 3.5: secure specialized and legacy systems against threats. These systems (operational technology, embedded devices, legacy gear) have critical roles and often can't be patched conventionally, so they need tailored controls, strong data handling, and regulatory compliance. The section covers specialized systems, OT, legacy characteristics, security practices, data management, compliance, and the challenges facing critical, commercial, and government services.

area 01

The Systems

Specialized systems (SoC, embedded, IoT, RF), operational technology (HVAC, ICS, SCADA), and the characteristics of legacy systems.

area 02

Protecting Them

Security practices (segmentation, hardening, monitoring), data management (aggregation, analytics), compliance (regulatory, environmental, safety).

area 03

The Stakes

Critical services challenges (utilities, transport, healthcare) and commercial/government challenges (manufacturing, finance, defense).
