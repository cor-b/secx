Incident Response

Preparing for incidents, acting fast when they hit, investigating what happened, and learning from it so it does not happen again.

overview

This section spans Domain 1 (Governance, Risk, Compliance, OBJ 1.2: perform risk management activities) and Domain 4 (Security Operations, OBJ 4.4: analyze data and artifacts in support of incident response). Effective IR minimizes the impact of a breach and speeds recovery: practice with preparedness exercises, take coordinated immediate action to contain a threat, investigate the event, attribute the source, and run root cause analysis to close the gap for good.

phase 01

Prepare

Preparedness exercises (tabletop, walkthrough, parallel, simulation) rehearse the plan before a real incident.

phase 02

Respond

Immediate response contains and mitigates: crisis management for communication, threat response for containment.

phase 03

Investigate

Event response reconstructs the timeline, recovers data, and runs breach response after containment.

phase 04

Learn

Attribution identifies who did it; root cause analysis finds why, feeding fixes back into planning.
