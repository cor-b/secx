Threat Modeling Considerations

Knowing the adversary: threat actor motivation, resources, and capabilities, plus attack patterns, methods, and the full attack lifecycle from initial access to evasion.

overview

Domains 1 & 3 OBJ 1.4 / OBJ 3.2: perform threat modeling and enhance endpoint/server security. Topics span threat actor motivation (geopolitical, espionage, financial, activism, notoriety), resources (time, money), and capabilities (knowledge, vulnerability/exploit creation, supply chain access), then attack patterns (injection, auth, on-path), threat actor methods (abuse cases, anti-patterns, attack trees), and the attack lifecycle: initial access and escalation (injection, credential dumping, privilege escalation) and post-exploitation/evasion (lateral movement, unauthorized execution, defensive evasion).
