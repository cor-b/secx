Artificial Intelligence

The information security challenges of AI adoption: generative AI, governance and legal/privacy risks, threats to the model, AI-enabled and manipulation attacks, usage risks, and AI bots.

overview

Domains 1 and 3 OBJ 1.5 / OBJ 3.6: summarize the information security challenges of AI adoption and use automation to secure the enterprise. Topics span generative AI (GPT, code assist, documentation), ethical and governance considerations (EU AI Act, organizational policies), legal and privacy risks (misuse, explainable vs non-explainable), threats to the model (prompt injection, poisoning, model theft/inversion), AI-enabled attacks (plugins, pipeline injectors, automated exploits), AI manipulation attacks (social engineering, deepfakes), AI usage risks (overreliance, disclosure, excessive agency), and AI bots (access, guardrails, DLP, disclosure).
