Threat Modeling Frameworks

Structured approaches to understanding and defending against attacks: the Cyber Kill Chain, CAPEC, MITRE ATT&CK, the Diamond Model, STRIDE, and OWASP.

overview

Domain 1 (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) OBJ 1.4: perform threat modeling activities. Topics span the Cyber Kill Chain (seven sequential attack phases), CAPEC (MITRE's attack-pattern catalog), MITRE ATT&CK (adversary TTP matrices and Navigator), the Diamond Model of Intrusion Analysis (adversary, infrastructure, capability, victim), STRIDE (six threat categories for secure design), and OWASP (Top 10, ASVS, ZAP for web application security).
